1 The Conference

This conference provides a platform for students to interact with professionals from various fields willing to share their unique experiences. Tech enthusiasts share their experiences and provide the beginners with a new vision towards technological advancement. The conference begins with keynote sessions by all the speakers, followed by an engaging panel discussion and a Q&A session. Not only this, curious students who are seen to be most involved in the conference will be given a chance for influential networking with the panel of speakers.

Previous Events
DEVFEST

DevFests are large, community-run developer events happening around the globe focused on community building and learning about Google’s technologies. DevFest’17 was our flagship event last year which turned out to be one of the biggest hackathon in VIT. The idea and implementation of all the teams was assessed at each stage and the best teams took home stellar prizes. Another part of the event was the ‘Ideathon’ held for freshers, with the intention of seeking the best business models for the most innovative ideas. The event was designed purely for the recognition of bright minds.

WTM

Women TechMakers is Google’s initiative for bringing more women into the technical ground and aims at creating a strong and independent global community of women. WTM VIT organised a hackathon called WomenTechies in March 2018 as a part of the International Women’s Day celebrations in VIT, which was the first ever hackathon exclusively for women to take part in and to be conducted in VIT. The event brought out a number of innovative projects and ideas to light made during the span of 12 hours of rigorous coding. This served as an encouragement to all women.

ANDROID STUDY JAMS

Developers Student Circle VIT took up the initiative of organising study sessions for the students of VIT, Vellore.The study sessions revolved around Android and its various applications.There were five comprehensive weekly sessions held with an introductory session on Android followed by sessions on different applications of Android building on the concepts taught earlier. The series was open to all and was aimed at equipping the attendees with technical knowledge that is crucial in the field of Android, at the end of which the participants were able to make basic Android apps.
